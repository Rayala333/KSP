About Kesupalli

Kesupalli is a Village in Enkoor Mandal in Khammam District of Telangana State, India. It belongs to Telangana region . It is located 37 KM towards East from District head quarters Khammam.

Kesupalli Pin code is 507168 and postal head office is Enkoor .

Kesupalli is surrounded by Julurupadu Mandal towards North , Thallada Mandal towards South , Konijerla Mandal towards west , Kallur Mandal towards South .

Palwancha , Kothagudem , Yellandu , Khammam are the near by Cities to Kesupalli.

Demographics of Kesupalli :

Telugu is the Local Language here. Total population of Kesupalli is 1343 .Males are 663 and Females are 680 living in 326 Houses. Total area of Kesupalli is 609 hectares..
